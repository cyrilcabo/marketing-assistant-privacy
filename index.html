Privacy Policy

Application: Marketing Assistant · Effective date: July 6, 2026

Marketing Assistant (the “App”) is a privately operated marketing-automation tool used by its operator to create, schedule, and publish marketing content to Facebook Pages that the operator personally administers, and to read the resulting post performance data. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

1. Who operates this App & scope of use

The App is operated by an individual (the “Operator”) for their own marketing use. It is not a public-facing consumer product. Questions may be directed to cyrilcabo@gmail.com.

Internal use only. The App is strictly for the Operator’s own internal use. It is not offered, licensed, sold, or made available to any other organization, business, or to any person not affiliated with the Operator. No third party is granted access to the App or to any data it processes, and the App does not authenticate or serve any end users other than the Operator.

2. Information the App accesses

Through Facebook Login and the Facebook Graph API, the App accesses only what is required to publish and measure content on Pages the Operator manages:

  • Facebook Page access tokens for Pages the Operator administers.
  • Page and post identifiers (Page IDs, post IDs) needed to create, schedule, update, or delete posts.
  • Post performance metrics (e.g. reach, reactions, comments, shares) returned by the Facebook Insights API for the Operator’s own posts.

The App does not collect, request, or store personal information about visitors to the Pages or about the general public. It does not use Facebook Login for any third-party end users.

3. How information is used

Accessed data is used solely to operate the App’s features for the Operator’s own Pages: publishing and scheduling content (text, photos, carousels, videos, and reels), keeping Facebook’s scheduler in sync, and reporting post performance back to the Operator.

4. How information is stored and protected

Facebook Page access tokens are stored encrypted at rest (AES-256-GCM) in a private database that is not publicly accessible. Tokens are never displayed in logs or shared externally.

5. Information sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or share any accessed data with third parties. Data is transmitted only between the App and Facebook’s own APIs.

6. Data retention and deletion

Page tokens and related records are retained only while the Operator keeps the Page connected to the App. When a Page is disconnected, or upon request, the associated token and records are deleted.

Data deletion requests: To have any data associated with this App deleted, email cyrilcabo@gmail.com with the subject line “Data Deletion Request”. Requests are actioned within 30 days. You may also revoke the App’s access at any time from your Facebook Settings → Business Integrations.

7. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date above reflects the latest revision.

8. Contact

For any questions about this policy, contact cyrilcabo@gmail.com.
